About Us

Vivek and Prem, brothers from Salem, Tamil Nadu, are the creators of Tamil Gaming, one of the first and most recognized Tamil-language gaming channels on YouTube. Since launching their channel in 2011, they’ve built a loyal community of gamers by delivering engaging gameplay videos, live streams, and commentary—all in authentic Tamil.
